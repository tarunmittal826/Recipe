Your Paneer kohlapuri recipe

A typical Maharashtrian taste profile, with very rich, complex spice notes, pe a crispy parantha or steamed rice, along with some amti dal or kachumber.

Total time: 50-55 minutes

Ingredients

  • Coconut: 1 cup (70 gms approx.), grated
  • Dry Red Chillies. 4 pieces
  • Onion 2, finely chopped
  • Paneer: 750 gms, cut into cubes
  • Caesar Dressing
